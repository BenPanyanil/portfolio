My customer projects

Päivystystilanteiden opas - Mobile PWA

Päivystystilanteiden opas - is a guidebook for evaluating a possible emergency situation available for the nurses in Helsinki. Assessing the situation involves measuring customer vital signs and detecting unusual findings. The on-call guide will then guide the nurse to respond appropriately to the situation. Now that the world has gone digital, the guide has also found its place in the binary space.

I had the privilege to design the UX and the UI, together with the local social health professionals and Futurice’s lead designer as my mentor. Back at the office we build the thing with React and made it a PWA.

3X10D - Youth well-being survey Webapp

3x10D is an online survey WebApp owned by Diakonia ammattiopisto (vocational school). The app was created to survey the well-being of young people in Finland. The application consists of two parts, the survey and the survey manager. I got to design the UI's for both survey respondent side of the app and the manager side. My work also included app testing on various devices and bug hunting.
